## Smiley v5.0.11 🎉

**Critical Mac fix** — stops system freezes caused by now-playing poll floods.

Made by [@1tsRaj](https://github.com/1tsRaj).

### What's fixed

- 🛑 **Mac freeze fix** — throttled now-playing polls (15s on macOS), removed zero-delay presence/IPC floods
- ⚡ **Safer music sync** — Discord RPC throttled to 5s, renderer updates to 1.5s
- 🔧 **Release CI fix** — audit step no longer blocks on dev-only dependency warnings

### If Smiley freezes your Mac before updating

1. Force quit Smiley (Activity Monitor)
2. Turn off **Show current song** in Settings, or launch with `SMILEY_DISABLE_NOW_PLAYING=1 open -a Smiley`
