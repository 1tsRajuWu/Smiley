## Smiley v6.0.4 🎉

**Patch release** — faster now-playing updates and cleaner listening presence.

Made by [@1tsRaj](https://github.com/1tsRaj).

### What's fixed

- 🎵 **Faster track changes** — macOS polls every ~2.5s while listening; Discord text updates immediately on title/artist change (artwork follows async)
- 🚫 **No music progress bar** — removed preview progress bar and Discord elapsed/remaining timer for listening activity
- 🎧 **Song info only** — Discord shows track title + artist/album, no playback time bar
