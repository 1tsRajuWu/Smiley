How it works

Three steps. Zero config.

1

Download

Installer for Windows, .dmg for Mac, AppImage or .deb for Linux.

2

Open Discord

Desktop app required — browser Discord won't show Rich Presence.

3

Pick an activity

Gaming, listening, or chilling — your profile updates instantly.
