Features

Quiet power. Clear presence.

Everything you need to show what you’re actually doing — without turning Discord into a project.

Seven first-class modes

Gaming, coding, music, food, chilling, anime, and studying — each with room for detail, not just a vague status.

Local, instant updates

Switch activities from the desktop app. Your profile updates without developer portals or hand-written JSON.

Cross-platform by default

Native-feeling installs for Windows, Apple Silicon, Intel Mac, and Linux — same product, same polish.

Designed for all-day use

Not a gamer-only overlay. Carry one presence language from deep work through lunch, anime, and late-night chill.

Expressive when you want it

Custom activities and animated flair stay optional. The default experience stays calm and intentional.

Free and open

MIT-licensed. Fork-friendly. Built for people who care how their profile looks — without a subscription.
